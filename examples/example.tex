Hirzebruch--Riemann--Roch \cite[Thm.~A.4.1]{har77} states that for a locally free sheaf $\mathscr{E}$ of rank $r$ on a nonsingular projective variety $X$ of dimension $n$,
\[ \chi(\mathscr{E})=\deg(\operatorname{ch}(\mathscr{E}).\operatorname{td}(\mathscr{T}))_{n}, \]
where $(\mbox{ })_{n}$ denotes the component of degree $n$ in $A(X)\otimes \mathbb{Q}$.
